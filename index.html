About us

Independent. Family Run. Members Only.

Cube and Triangle is an independent, family run members' only club in the heart of Lincoln. Two floors, 40 Silver Street — two minutes from the Stonebow, a world away from the high street.

Whether it's a competitive frame of snooker, a darts night with mates, or somewhere to take someone you want to impress — this is the place. Great food, a well-stocked bar, and Sky and BT Sport across 14 screens.

Our Pledge

Sustainability

At Cube and Triangle, we recognise that our actions have an impact on the environment, and we are dedicated to reducing that impact. We promote practices that have a positive impact on the environment and continually seek innovative ways to be as sustainable as possible.

Read our full environmental pledge
1

Reduce Waste

We minimise waste through recycling. Our waste handlers are zero landfill. We no longer serve food with any single-use plastic. We only stock Teapigs Tea as the tea bag is compostable. Salad food waste is limited — the guinea pigs at home happily consume unused salad. Vinegar sachets are available on request only. Tap water is available on request. Our bottled water comes in 100% rPET packaging.

2

Source Locally

We prioritise sourcing ingredients from local, sustainable, and ethical suppliers wherever possible. This supports our community and reduces our carbon footprint. Many of our drinks are distilled and bottled in the UK, reducing their air-miles.

3

Climate-Friendly Drinks

Some of our drinks actively protect the environment. Hawksbill Rum donates proceeds to protect the Hawksbill Turtle. Molson Coors Coastal Wine is in partnership with Plastic Oceans Org. Sapling Vodka plants a tree with every bottle sale. We plan to stock Toti Dark Rum, which gives proceeds to marine projects.

4

Fair-Trade Products

Stokes Coffee is Fair-Trade and aligned with the Rainforest Alliance. Fair coffee liqueur is made from Fair-Trade coffee beans. We also stock fair-trade sugar.

5

Plant-Based Options

We offer a variety of plant-based menu options. We do not serve seafood due to its environmental impact on the ocean. We only serve oat milk. Many of our drinks are vegan and our creamy liqueurs are all plant-based.

6

Conserve Water & Energy

We reduce consumption through efficient equipment including our new glasswasher and LED lighting. We use an app to ensure equipment is only on when necessary. Taps in the toilets automatically turn off. Our energy supply is 100% renewable.
