Club conduct

House Rules

Our rules exist to make sure everyone has a great time. Please take a moment to read them.

This page is being updated by the club. Full house rules will be published here shortly. In the meantime, please speak to staff at the bar if you have any questions.

General Conduct

  • ✓All members and guests are expected to treat staff and fellow members with courtesy and respect.
  • ✓Management reserve the right to refuse entry or membership at their discretion.
  • ✓Members are responsible for the conduct of any guests they sign in.
  • ✓Aggressive, threatening or abusive behaviour will result in immediate removal and potential membership termination.

Equipment & Tables

  • ✓Please treat all equipment with care. Damage caused deliberately or through negligence may be charged to the responsible member.
  • ✓Tables are booked per hour. Please vacate promptly when your booking ends if others are waiting.
  • ✓Food and drink must be kept away from the table cloth. Use the provided surfaces.

Membership

  • ✓Membership is non-transferable. Your membership card or account may not be used by any other person.
  • ✓Members must have been paid-up for at least 24 hours before signing in guests.
  • ✓Membership may be suspended or revoked for serious or repeated breach of club rules.
